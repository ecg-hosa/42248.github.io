Meeting Notes for Weds/9/24/20

If you missed our meeting last Wednesday, don't worry! Here you can view our meeting powerpoint, which goes over the bare bones of our discussion.

In summary, we discussed which types of competitive events you could choose. This chart might help you decide. The Piedmont-Triad regional/district competition is now scheduled for January 2021, so be sure to register as a member if you want to participate! Our HOSA registration deadline is October 2nd. There is an additional $15 competition fee for the regional competition, along with the $21 membership, so keep that in mind when you are deciding whether or not to compete. IF YOU DON'T WANT TO COMPETE, YOU DON'T HAVE TO BECOME A NC HOSA MEMBER! You can still participate in ECG HOSA club events without becoming an official NC HOSA member.
