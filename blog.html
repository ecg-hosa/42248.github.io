Meeting Notes for Weds/9/24/20

If you missed our meeting last Wednesday, don't worry! Here you can view our meeting powerpoint, which goes over the bare bones of our discussion.


In summary, we discussed which types of competitive events you could choose. This chart might help you decide, but don't forget to take a look at event guidelines.
Online testing for the Piedmont-Triad regional/district competition is now scheduled for January 18-27, 2021, so register as a member if you want to participate! Our HOSA registration deadline is October 2nd. There is an additional $15 competition fee for the regional competition, along with the $21 membership, so keep that in mind when you are deciding if you want to compete. Regionals is not required to compete at states for most events. We will be sending out a Google Form about which events you want to do once membership is finalized.
If you don't want to compete at any level (regional, state, or international), you don't have to complete the form or pay the fee! You can still participate in ECG HOSA club events without becoming an official NC HOSA member.


Here is the link to the Zoom recording of the meeting! The password is f1kP#mZX. You can watch it if you'd like; it's about 20 minutes long.

TL;DR

  • Register to become a member ($21) if you want to compete.
  • Regionals are in January and has a $15 fee.
  • Start thinking about which competitive events you want to do.
