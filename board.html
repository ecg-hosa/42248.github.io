Interested in having a board position?

Elections are held annually in April. ECG HOSA board members are chosen by a hybrid system that combines Votes with merit. Candidates are ranked for position fit, club involvment, and popular vote. This ensures that board members will be both fairly elected and well-prepared for their position.
