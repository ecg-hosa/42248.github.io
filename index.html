We are HOSA,


A Health Careers Club at the Early College at Guilford


HOSA stands for Health Occupation Students of America. Our chapter at ECG competes in the annual North Carolina HOSA State Leadership Conference. As a health and medical-focused organization, we are also passionate about our service to the community.

Leadership Conferences

ECG competes at three different levels: Regional, State, and International.
